Our Story

Rooted in Good Food

From a single market stall in 2018 to Nairobi's go-to organic destination — here's how we got here.

How It Started

A Market Stall in Westlands

Kale & Co. began in 2018 when founder Njambi Mwangi set up a small table at the Westlands Organic Market, selling surplus produce from her family's farm in Thika. Within three months, she was the first seller to sell out every single Sunday.

By 2020, Njambi had leased a 600 sq ft shopfront on Ring Road. By 2022, that had grown to a 2,000 sq ft store with a café counter at the back — the space where regulars now spend entire Sunday mornings with a flat white and the newspaper.

Today, Kale & Co. stocks over 200 certified-organic products, works directly with 40+ Kenyan farmers, and serves roughly 300 customers a week in-store.

What Drives Us

Three Things We Never Compromise On

🌍

Traceability

Every product on our shelf has a name and a postcode behind it. We visit every supplier at least twice a year.

🤲

Fair Pay

We pay farmers 20–30% above market rate and commit to minimum purchase agreements so they can plan ahead.

♻️

Zero Waste

Unsold produce goes to a local children's home. Our packaging is 100% compostable or reusable glass.
