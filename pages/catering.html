What We Offer

Catering Packages

Every event is different — so every menu is built from scratch. Here's what we specialise in.

🥂 Brunch Spreads

Grazing tables, fresh juice stations, artisan coffee bars and wholesome hot plates. Perfect for morning corporate events, baby showers and weekend celebrations up to 80 guests.

🍽️ Sit-Down Dinners

Plated three-course meals using seasonal organic ingredients. We work with you to design a menu that reflects your event — from intimate dinners of 10 to formal sit-downs of 60.

🥗 Grazing & Bowl Stations

Interactive food stations where guests build their own bowls from a curated selection of grains, roasted vegetables, proteins and dressings. A crowd favourite at corporate events and team offsites.

The Process

How It Works

01

Get in Touch

Tell us your event date, guest count, venue and any dietary requirements. We'll get back to you within 24 hours.

02

Menu Planning

We'll design a custom menu based on your theme, season and preferences. A tasting session can be arranged for larger events.

03

Confirmation

Once you're happy with the menu and quote, we lock in your date with a 30% deposit. The rest is paid on delivery.

04

We Show Up

Our team arrives early, sets up, serves your guests and clears up afterwards. You enjoy the event — we handle everything else.
