Ordering & Delivery

Yes! We deliver across Nairobi including Westlands, Kilimani, Karen, Lavington and the CBD. Orders placed before 11:00 AM are delivered the same afternoon by 5:00 PM. Delivery costs KSh 200, or free on orders over KSh 3,000.

The minimum order is KSh 1,500. This helps us keep delivery sustainable and ensures your produce stays fresh in a full insulated bag.

Currently we take delivery orders via WhatsApp at +254 712 345 678. Send us your list, delivery address and preferred time. We'll confirm availability and send an M-Pesa payment prompt.
Organic & Sourcing

We only source from farms certified by the Kenya Organic Agriculture Network (KOAN). We also conduct unannounced farm visits at least twice a year and require suppliers to share soil and pesticide testing records.

Yes, for items that can't be grown locally like olive oil, quinoa and certain spices. We source these from certified organic cooperatives and import directly — no middlemen. All imported items are clearly labelled with their country of origin.

The Café

The café is open Monday to Saturday 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM and Sunday 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM. The kitchen closes 30 minutes before we do — last food orders at 4:30 PM (3:30 PM Sundays).
